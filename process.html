Our Process

  1. Discovery Consultation – We discuss your vision, style preferences, budget and timeline.
  2. On-Site Visit – We visit your property, take measurements, evaluate the space and gather inspiration.
  3. Design Proposal – You receive mood boards, layouts, furniture selections and a complete design direction.
  4. Approval & Paperwork – Once the proposal is approved and agreements are signed, we begin sourcing and scheduling.
  5. Procurement & Installation – We coordinate ordering, deliveries, installation and final styling.
  6. Final Reveal – Walk into a completed home designed around your lifestyle.
