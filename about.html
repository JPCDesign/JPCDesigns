About JPC Design

Thoughtful interiors with a warm modern point of view.

JPC Design helps homeowners turn empty, unfinished or disconnected spaces into homes that feel complete, personal and comfortable.

Design philosophy

Every piece should earn its place.

Furniture scale, lighting temperature, fabrics, art, window treatments and decor are selected together so the home feels intentional instead of collected at random.

Personal

The design direction starts with how you live and what each room needs to do.

Cohesive

Rooms are planned together so materials, colors and forms connect naturally.

Practical

Selections consider comfort, maintenance, budget and timelines.
